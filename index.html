About Naruto

A boy born as a vessel for a demon, shunned by his village, rising to become the greatest Hokage — the story of Naruto Uzumaki is one of the most beloved in all of manga and anime.

The Manga

Written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, Naruto ran in Weekly Shōnen Jump from 1999 to 2014 — 700 chapters across 72 volumes, selling over 250 million copies worldwide.

The Anime

The original Naruto series aired 220 episodes (2002–2007). Naruto: Shippuden followed with 500 episodes (2007–2017), adapting the second half of the manga with expanded battles and filler arcs.

The World

Set in a fictional world where nations field armies of shinobi — ninja who manipulate chakra to perform jutsu. The story centers on the Hidden Leaf Village and its surrounding world of five great nations.

The Hero

Naruto Uzumaki, jinchuriki of the Nine-Tailed Fox, goes from the village outcast to the Seventh Hokage through sheer willpower, compassion, and a refusal to give up on anyone — even his enemies.

The Chakra System

Chakra is the core power system — a mix of physical and spiritual energy that flows through the body. Shinobi mold chakra using hand seals to perform ninjutsu, genjutsu, and taijutsu techniques.

Legacy

Naruto spawned the sequel series Boruto, dozens of films, video games, merchandise, and a global fanbase. It remains one of the best-selling manga series of all time alongside One Piece and Dragon Ball.

Naruto's Journey

01

The Academy Years

Naruto fails the graduation exam three times but learns the Shadow Clone Jutsu, passes, and joins Team 7 under Kakashi Hatake with Sasuke and Sakura.

02

Chunin Exams & Orochimaru

Team 7 enters the exams alongside other genin. Orochimaru marks Sasuke with the cursed seal, and the village is invaded — setting up Sasuke's eventual departure.

03

Training Under Jiraiya

After Sasuke defects, Naruto trains with Jiraiya for 2.5 years, mastering his chakra control and Rasengan refinement in preparation for Akatsuki's growing threat.

04

Shippuden — The Akatsuki Threat

Naruto returns. The Akatsuki hunts all tailed beasts. Gaara is captured, Deidara and Sasori are defeated. The scope of the conflict expands dramatically.

05

Pain's Assault & Sage Mode

Naruto learns Sage Mode from the toads. He faces Pain alone after Konoha is destroyed, defeats all six paths, and meets Nagato — choosing to break the cycle of hate.

06

Fourth Shinobi World War & Beyond

The Allied Shinobi Forces face Madara, Obito, and the Ten-Tails. Naruto masters his Nine-Tails chakra, achieves Six Paths Sage Mode, and finally reconciles with Sasuke.
